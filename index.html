About East African Coffee Fest

East Africa’s Premier Coffee Festival – Explore, Learn, Network & Celebrate Coffee Culture!

Experience the Ultimate Coffee Adventure Across Kenya, Tanzania, Uganda, Burundi, Rwanda & The Democratic Republic of Congo

The East African Coffee Fest 2025 is a year-long journey celebrating the rich coffee culture of Kenya and Uganda. Join coffee lovers, brewers, exhibitors, and industry enthusiasts for an immersive experience filled with discovery, business opportunities, and adventure.

  • Travel across vibrant cities in Kenya, Tanzania & Uganda, experiencing coffee like never before.
  • Engage in hands-on workshops, tasting sessions, and expert-led discussions.
  • Network with top coffee brands, professionals, and fellow enthusiasts to build lasting connections and close business deals.

East African Coffee Fest Events

Join us for an unforgettable coffee experience in different locations throughout the year!

1

Wundanyi - 27th Feb-1st March

A Taste of Tradition in the Hills

Join us in the breathtaking highlands of Wundanyi for an exclusive coffee experience! Discover the rich flavors of Taita Hills coffee, meet local farmers, and enjoy barista showcases, cupping sessions, and farm tours. Don't miss this chance to explore the roots of Kenyan coffee culture.

2

Naivasha - 20th-23rd March

Savor the Brew by the Lake

Experience the magic of coffee in Naivasha, set against the stunning backdrop of Lake Naivasha. Enjoy artisan coffee tastings, specialty brewing workshops, and networking with top coffee producers. A must-attend event for coffee lovers and industry professionals!

3

Mombasa - 17th-20th April

Coffee Meets the Coastline

Indulge in a unique coffee experience by the Indian Ocean! From beachside espresso bars to cold brew innovations, this festival brings the best of Kenyan coffee to the coast. Experience live music, coffee cocktails, and a deep dive into Swahili coffee traditions.

4

Eldoret - 5th-7th June

High-Altitude Brews & Business

Set in the heart of Kenya’s Rift Valley, this summit is perfect for coffee traders, roasters, and enthusiasts. Learn about high-altitude coffee farming, emerging trends, and business opportunities in the global coffee market. Don't miss expert panels and live roasting demos!

5

Mbale, UG - 31st July-2nd Aug

Where Coffee & Culture Collide

Step into Uganda’s vibrant coffee culture in Mbale! Experience the finest Ugandan Arabica, traditional brewing methods, and an immersive journey through coffee history. Engage with farmers and baristas while enjoying local music, art, and food pairings..

6

Nairobi - 28th-30th Aug

The Heartbeat of African Coffee

Nairobi, the hub of East Africa’s coffee industry, hosts this high-energy event featuring barista championships, new product launches, and expert-led seminars. Whether you're a coffee aficionado or an industry player, this is the place to be!

7

Moshi, TZ - 2nd-4th Oct

Coffee at the Foot of Kilimanjaro

Journey to the base of Mount Kilimanjaro and experience Tanzania’s world-renowned coffee. Explore the link between coffee and conservation, visit estates growing Kilimanjaro coffee, and enjoy thrilling adventure activities like hiking and safaris.

8

Embu - 20th-23rd Nov

Celebrating the Coffee Harvest

Embu, one of Kenya’s premier coffee-growing regions, comes alive for this harvest festival! Engage in farm tours, coffee processing workshops, and taste fresh seasonal brews. Celebrate with local farmers and industry experts in this unforgettable event.

9

Diani - 18th-20th Dec

A Coastal Coffee Celebration

Unwind in Diani with coffee tastings, sunset beach sessions, and innovative coffee cocktails. Enjoy the perfect blend of relaxation and coffee culture, featuring live acoustic music, expert talks, and a showcase of Kenya’s finest coastal coffee blends.

Key Highlights

The East Africa Coffee Fest is an immersive event celebrating coffee, tea, wine, and the broader hospitality industry. It offers a platform where passion meets expertise, bringing together farmers, producers, investors, hospitality professionals, and enthusiasts.

Investors’ Dinner & Tasting

Indulge in a premium selection of coffee, tea, and wine while connecting with top hotel owners, managers, and hospitality professionals.

Marketplace & Exhibitions

Discover innovative businesses from industries like hospitality, finance, fashion, logistics, technology, health, and education.

Educational Sessions

Gain insights from industry leaders, enhance your skills with specialized training, and explore career opportunities in the coffee sector.

Live Demonstrations & Competitions

Experience thrilling competitions featuring baristas, mixologists, sommeliers, and service professionals as they showcase their skills for cash prizes.

SMEs Pitch Deck

Learn how to develop business plans, master digital marketing, and network with investors. Top prizes: 10,000, 7,000, and 3,000 KES. Registration: 2,000 KES.

Basic to Masterclass Training

Comprehensive coffee industry training covering business strategies, hands-on skills, and industry insights. Fees: 1 session - 1,000 KES, 4 sessions - 4,000 KES, Full package - 6,500 KES.
