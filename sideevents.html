Side Events For The East African Coffee Fest

Whether you're a coffee expert or just curious, the East African Coffee fest blends education, skills development, and enjoyment.Don't miss out!

Women In Coffee Value Chain

The East African coffee fest want to include the ladies and celebrate the role of women in changing the coffee industry. To that effect, we will lauch the Women in Coffee Value chain chapter in Wundanyi-Taita Taveta between 27 th February and 1 st March, 2025 at Taita Rocks Hotel.Join us to usher in a new era of women in coffee and enjoy the many benefits that come with it.

  • 1 Free Masterclass session on coffee value chain
  • Panel discussions on empowerment & innovation.
  • Networking opportunities with industry experts.
  • Attend the launching of women in coffee chapter in Wundanyi, Taita Taveta.
  • Connection to regional and international associations/organization.

Empowering Coffee Communities Through CSR Initiatives

The East African Coffee Fest is dedicated to supporting and uplifting the communities integral to our coffee industry.

  • Educational Bursaries: Providing scholarships to students from Kenyan coffee farming regions to foster education and community development.
  • Support for Displaced Farmers: Assisting displaced coffee farmers from the SOPACDI Cooperative in South Kivu, DRC, to help them rebuild their livelihoods.

Youth and Coffee Kids Program- Chapter Launch

Empowering the next generation in the Coffee Industry (ages 14-30)

  • Gain access to mentorship and skills development to build sustainable and profitable coffee businesses.
  • Skill development about coffee processing & business strategies.
  • Networking sessions with industry experts, investors & fellow youth.
  • Get mentorship & explore career paths in coffee.
  • Attend the launching of youth and coffee kids program chapter.

Meet the Farmers

The East African Coffee Fest offers you a chance for a real world interaction with the first stage of the coffee production process.

Engage on candid conversations with the people involved in feeding the nation ,participate in provision and planting of Coffee seedlings and indigenous trees to farmers to help in conservation of the environment in Taita Hills , tree planting and support conservation efforts in Taita Hills.
